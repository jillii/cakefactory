Cake factory is a Boston-based collective and label that hosts techno shows around Boston and releases music semi-regularly.

Cake Factory is focused on the cultivation of local, underground techno and ambient music in Boston, MA. We are actively seeking submissions from Boston-based techno for future events. POC, LGBTQ+, non-binary, and/or female-identifying artists are particularly encouraged to submit their work.

If you would like to be considered for upcoming events (or if you know someone that you think should be considered), please submit a link to your/their website that includes a representative recording. If we think your/their music would be a good fit, we will respond as soon as we can about potential dates. We're a small operation though and appreciate your patience.

Techno music submissions: berwickcakefactory@gmail.com
